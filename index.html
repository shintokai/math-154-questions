Welcome

This website provides practice questions for MATH 154.

The goal is to support independent learning through guided practice, targeted feedback, and optional video explanations.

How to Use This Website

Please follow the steps below when working through the practice questions:

  1. Attempt the problem independently.
    Do not view the answer or solution first. Active recall is the most effective way to build mastery.
  2. Consider question difficulty.
    Questions are labeled Easy, Intermediate, or Difficult to help you select appropriate practice or increase challenge progressively.
  3. Check your answer.
    Click “Answer” to reveal the expected result.
  4. Review the full solution if needed.
    If your answer is incorrect — or you are unsure how to proceed — click “Full Solution” to view a detailed explanation.
  5. Watch video explanations when available.
    Some questions include short solution videos. These are useful if you would like additional clarification, especially after reviewing the written solution.
