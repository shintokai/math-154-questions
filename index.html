Welcome

This website provides curated practice questions for MATH 154. It’s built to help you strengthen your understanding through structured practice, clear solutions, and optional video support.

You can also download full practice sets as PDF files for offline work. These are the same questions you’ll find on the site and are ideal for printing or working on a tablet.

How to Use This Website

To get the most out of these practice questions, try the steps below:

  1. Attempt each question first.
    Try the problem before checking the answer. Working through the idea yourself is the most effective way to learn.
  2. Use the difficulty levels to guide your practice.
    Questions are labeled Easy, Intermediate, or Challenging. Start where you feel comfortable and move up as you gain confidence.
  3. Check your answer.
    Click “Answer” to compare your work with the expected result.
  4. Review the full solution if needed.
    If your approach doesn’t match or you’re unsure about a step, open the “Full Solution” for a complete explanation.
  5. Use the video explanations when helpful.
    Some questions include short videos that go through the reasoning — especially useful if you prefer visual or verbal explanations.
  6. Refer to the Learning Outcomes.
    Each outcome lists exactly which questions correspond to that topic. Use this page to target specific skills before quizzes, midterms, or finals. It’s also helpful if you want to review a topic you’re struggling with.
  7. Download PDFs if you prefer offline practice.
    All questions are available as printable PDFs on the PDF page.
