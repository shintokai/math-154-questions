Welcome

This website provides curated practice questions for MATH 154. It is designed to help you build confidence and deepen your understanding through structured practice, immediate feedback, and optional video support.

You may also download full practice sets as PDF files for offline work. These PDFs contain the same questions and are ideal for printing or tablet use.

How to Use This Website

To get the most from these questions, follow the steps below:

  1. Try each problem first.
    Avoid looking at the answer before attempting the question — active problem-solving is one of the most effective ways to learn.
  2. Use difficulty levels to guide you.
    Problems are labeled Easy, Intermediate, or Difficult. Start where you feel comfortable and increase the challenge at your own pace.
  3. Check your work.
    Click “Answer” to compare your result with the expected answer.
  4. Review worked solutions if needed.
    If your answer is incorrect, or if you feel unsure about your reasoning, click “Full Solution” for a detailed walkthrough.
  5. Watch optional video explanations.
    Many questions include short videos offering further explanation — helpful especially if you learn best through visual or verbal instruction.
  6. Download PDFs if preferred.
    Full practice sets are available for download on the PDF page, so you can work offline, print them, or annotate digitally.
